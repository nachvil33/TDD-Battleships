Da Rules

Welcome to the Battleships game! Here are da rules:

  • Each player has a 10x10 board with ships.
  • Place your ships on the board
  • Take turns shooting at the enemy board.
  • A hit is marked with red, a miss with white.
  • Sink all enemy ships to win.
